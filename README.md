## letstune :sparkles:

I'm the author of the [*letstune*](https://letstune.org/) package.

[<img src="https://raw.githubusercontent.com/mslapek/letstune/main/img/logo.svg" alt="drawing" width="400"/>](https://letstune.org/)

*The new standard* for ML training!

It's compatible with popular :snake: Python ML libraries.
This gives *an easy migration path* for users.

*letstune* automatically allocates resources to the
most promising models, which can :star2: **decrease
training time even 8×!** [^1] :star2:

[^1]: See fig. 8; "CIFAR10 Using Small Cuda Convnet Model" in [arXiv:1810.05934](https://arxiv.org/abs/1810.05934). Compare *Test Error*=0.25 for *Random* and *Hyperband (by rung)*.

By default, *letstune* executes training
on the local computer.

*letstune* has an elastic architecture -
it can be easily integrated with:

* your cloud,
* your on-premises infrastructure.

## About me :tiger:

Pronouns: He/Him

### ML + DDD experience

In my previous workplace, we have been
processing satellite and drone photos
for the agricultural sector.

Many of these systems were developed in Python
according to **Domain-driven design** :boom: (DDD) [^3]
and had *hexagonal architecture*.
It was motivated by the fact that these pipelines were
non-trivial:

- time-series of photos,
- data acquisition from commercial satellite photo providers,
- horror with georeferenced data and so on.

[^3]: I've even *studied*
[the book from Eric Evans](https://www.amazon.com/Domain-Driven-Design-Tackling-Complexity-Software/dp/0321125215). :joy:

**I was using talks with the Product Manager**
as the basis for the *architecture of the project* and *Jira tasks*.
Projects were developed in *an agile way*,
so feedback from product usage
facilitated cooperation with the Product Manager.

I enjoyed transforming *the business vision* into the software! :star:

*letstune* was inspired by my commercial experience
and the reactions of colleagues to the existing tooling.
It is intended to be useful for smaller organizations
(and big enterprises also! :wink:).
